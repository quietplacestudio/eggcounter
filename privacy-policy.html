Privacy Policy for Egg-Counter 1. Data Collection The "Egg-Counter" app is designed to respect your privacy. Local Data: All counts and sales data are stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you manually export it. No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers. 2. Third-Party Services Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you: Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect your Advertising ID to provide personalized ads. Google's Privacy Policy PayPal: When using the donation feature, you are redirected to the PayPal website. We do not store or see your credit card details or PayPal login credentials. PayPal Privacy Statement 3. Home-Widget The app includes a Home-Widget feature. To display your egg counts on the home screen, the app uses a background process that accesses the local database. No data is transmitted to external servers during this process. 4. Permissions INTERNET: Required to load advertisements and process donations via PayPal. AD_ID: Required by the Google Mobile Ads SDK. 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. 6.
